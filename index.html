About Me

Hi, I'm Ethan — a passionate event planner based in Los Angeles. I bring diverse experience across both B2B and B2C events, including trade show booth designs, college concerts, conventions, panels, and intimate socials. Whether it’s hosting a five-person coffee chat or creating a company booth seen by over 300,000 convention attendees, I’ve had the opportunity to do it all. My goal is to turn this passion into a full-time career—continuing to design meaningful experiences that connect and inspire communities.
